    About Us

    • Vision

    • Mission

    • Goals

    Math Ed Labs is an endeavour to manifest the true representation of hands-on and conceptually-driven Mathematics. It is an abode to nurture the knowledge, understanding & application that pertains to the subject, allowing learners to construct concepts that align with the curriculum.

    Math Ed Labs is working towards bringing a paradigm shift in the pedagogy of Mathematics. We set up a platform which ensures that extra space where the teachers and students get a chance to discover their abilities and improvise upon their teaching and learning skills.

    Our projects range from catering the need of early stage learners to middle graders with following goals

    • To improve mathematics learning environment in schools
    • To enrich students and teachers with a variety of mathematical resources
    • To transform the classroom transactions into joyful learning
    • To assist educational institutions in achieving national targets
